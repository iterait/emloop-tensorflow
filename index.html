    Welcome to cxflow-tensorflow docs¶

    cxflow-tensorflow is a cxflow plugin that allows you to train TensorFlow models effortlessly. Instead of having to write tens or hundreds of lines of code, cxflow-tensorflow lets you focus on the model architecture.

    However, before you start working with cxflow-tensorflow, make sure you have read

    • TensorFlow essentials
    • cxflow basics

    Once you’ve gone through that, you can proceed proceed with installation and go through our 5-minutes tutorial.

    Leveraging the speed and potential of multi-GPU models was never easier with our multi-GPU base model.
